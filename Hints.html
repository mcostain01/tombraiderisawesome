The original Tomb Raider game has many tips and tricks to help enhance your gameplay.


Secrets: There are multiple secrets in each level of the game. Each levels secrets varies, but the secrets range from ammo, medipaks and even weapons


Levels: There are 15 levels in total taking place in places like Peru, Greece, Egypt and Atlantis


Enemies: There are many different enemies in the game, all taking different methods in order to kill them. The bosses are a different category of enemy, and they are much harder to kill.
